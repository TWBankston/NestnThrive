What Makes Organization Actually Work?

The biggest mistake people make is buying containers before decluttering. You can't organize excess. True organization starts with editing your belongings down to what you use and love, then finding a home for what remains.

We prioritize "visible accessibility." If it takes three steps to put something away, you won't do it. Our guides focus on reducing friction so that staying organized is easier than being messy.
